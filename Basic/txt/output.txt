This is what we know about the apple history Apple Computer, Inc. was founded by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne on April 1, 1976. The company's first product was the Apple I, a motherboard with a processor and memory for hobbyists. The Apple I was sold without a keyboard, monitor, case, or other human interface devices. Jobs and Wozniak used their own resources to fund the company, with Jobs selling his Volkswagen minibus and Wozniak his programmable calculator..
